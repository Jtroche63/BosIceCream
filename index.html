Our Story

Since 1954, Bo's has been a beloved landmark in Tampa's Seminole Heights neighborhood. We've been serving generations of families with our classic soft-serve and signature creations, like the famous upside-down banana split.

We're dedicated to preserving the nostalgic walk-up window experience that has made us a community staple for over 60 years.
